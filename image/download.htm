THE BURGER HEARTS

Hamburgers
Sanriku CoastArea

American-style, locally produced, locally consumed hamburgers filled with Iwate

  • Tourist guide
  • Spot details
  • Access
  • Related information

Lunch plate includes French fries, mini salad, and a drink for +¥550.

The most popular item is the BH Cheeseburger Double (100% Iwate Shorthorn Wagyu Beef), a five-minute walk from Sanriku Railway Sakari Station. The restaurant's diner-style interior resembles a 1960s American hamburger store, with black and white tiled floors and bright red chairs, creating a retro-pop atmosphere. The main beef, pork, and chicken used in the burgers are all from Iwate Prefecture: Iwate Yamagata-mura Shorthorn Wagyu Beef, Kuroge Wagyu Beef Aged in Monzaki, Yongenton Pork Arisupoaku, and Nanbu Dori. The menu has more than 50 items, including a fried cod burger using Sanriku cod and a lamb burger unique to the restaurant, etc. The owner's wife has handmade eraser stamps on the flags attached to the burgers and hand-drawn illustrations are on the menu. hand-drawn illustrations on the menu.

The juicy, lean Shorthorn beef and the tried-and-true bun are an amazingly good match, and the only word that comes to mind is "delicious!

I am happiest now that I can do the work I love in the place where I was born and raised! Mr. Kikuchi, the owner of the restaurant, with a big smile on his face, holding a burger he is proud of.

The owner's personality seems to draw people in, and the store has become a place of relaxation in the community.
